Productivity tips

  • Use Pomodoro technique — 25 min study + 5 min break.
  • Review yesterday's notes for 10 min before starting new topics.
  • Write short one-line summaries after every lecture.
  • Lab programs: code, run, and explain to yourself out loud.
  • Set a weekly target and review your progress every Sunday.
